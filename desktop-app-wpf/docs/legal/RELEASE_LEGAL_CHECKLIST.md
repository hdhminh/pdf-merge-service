# Release Legal Checklist

1. Verify all NuGet dependency licenses and versions.
2. Verify icon/font/graphic assets have commercial usage rights.
3. Revalidate ngrok Terms of Service for current release usage.
4. Bundle `THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.txt` with release artifact.
5. Bundle `EULA.txt` and `PRIVACY_NOTE.txt` with release artifact.
6. Confirm app metadata (version, product, copyright).
7. Confirm no proprietary tokens/secrets are bundled.
8. Legal sign-off completed before publish.
