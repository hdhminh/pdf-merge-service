# DMCA Takedown Template

Use this template when another repository/site copies code or binaries from this
project without authorization.

## Email Subject

DMCA Takedown Notice - Unauthorized Use of Copyrighted Work

## Template

```text
To: [Service Provider / Platform Legal Contact]
Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]

I am the copyright owner (or authorized agent) of the software project below:

- Original work: [Project name]
- Original URL: [GitHub repository URL]
- Owner name: [Your legal name]
- Contact email: [Your email]

I have identified infringing material hosted on your service:

- Infringing URL(s):
  1) [URL]
  2) [URL]
- Description of infringing material:
  [Explain what was copied: source code, binaries, docs, branding, etc.]

Good faith statement:
I have a good faith belief that use of the copyrighted material described above
is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

Accuracy and authority statement:
The information in this notice is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, I am
the owner, or am authorized to act on behalf of the owner, of the exclusive
right that is allegedly infringed.

Requested action:
Please remove or disable access to the infringing material as soon as possible.

Electronic signature:
[Full legal name]
```

## Evidence Checklist

- Link to original repository/tag/release.
- Commit hashes with timestamp proving prior authorship.
- Screenshots or side-by-side diff.
- Build artifact names/checksums if binaries are copied.

## Notes

- Keep all communication archived.
- If the platform provides a web form, copy this template into that form.
- This template is operational guidance, not legal advice.
